A Guide to Smart Cards in the West Midlands

  1. 40,000 swift users across all modes of transport making more than 1.6 million journeys a month.
  2. There are currently 16.5 million smart cards in operation around the UK.
  3. 47% of customers use smart ticketing as their main option for journeys.
  4. Over 120 Members, including operators, government bodies and suppliers.
  5. The average amount of time people spend on public transport is 94 minutes. Over 95% of those riders spend more than 2 hours on public transport every day.

The purpose of this website is to encourage the public to travel via smart card on transport.
Smart Cards in the West Midlands started primarily on buses but has expanded to rail travel as well most recently.
